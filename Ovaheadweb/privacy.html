Privacy Policy

OvaHead (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website https://ovahead.com.

Information We Collect

  • Email addresses you provide when signing up for our waitlist or newsletter.
  • Usage data collected automatically through Google Analytics 4 (GA4), including page views, clicks, and interactions.
  • Information collected through third-party services we use, such as Formspree for form submissions.
  • IP addresses and browser information for analytics and site improvement purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you updates and newsletters if you signed up.
  • To improve our website, monitor usage, and track interactions through GA4.
  • To respond to your questions or requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce our policies.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services, including:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4): Collects anonymized usage data to improve our site.
  • Formspree: Handles email submissions for our waitlist form.
  • Social media links (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter) may be tracked for analytics.

Your Rights

  • You can unsubscribe from our emails at any time by using the link in the email.
  • You can request deletion of your personal data by emailing us at social@ovahead.com.
  • You have the right to know what data we collect and how it is used.

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and tracking technologies for analytics and site improvements. GA4 collects anonymized user interactions to help us understand how people engage with our site.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at social@ovahead.com.
